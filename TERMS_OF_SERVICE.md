## About Sila

Sila is a client implementation for the Sila blockchain’s consensus protocol. To participate in the network, a user must send SILA from the Sila mainnet blockchain to a validator deposit smart contract on Sila mainnet. Validators participate in proposing and voting on blocks in the protocol, and the network applies rewards/penalties based on their behavior. A detailed set of installation and usage instructions as well as breakdowns of each individual component are available in the official documentation portal, however, we do not warrant the accuracy, completeness or usefulness of this documentation.
